NO 4

NO 4 is a Saffron, Oud, Vanilla is a Amber Spicy fragrance for women and men. NO 4 Saffron, Oud, Vanilla was launched in 2015. The nose behind this fragrance is Rosendo Mateu. Top notes are Bitter Orange Blossom and Calabrian bergamot; middle notes are Saffron, Jasmine, Nutmeg, Cinnamon and Iris; base notes are Agarwood (Oud), Vanilla, Vetiver, Sandalwood, Guaiac Wood, Benzoin, Tonka Bean, Patchouli, Amber and Labdanum
